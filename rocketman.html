Biden Bot

This is a platformer game coded in C3 and Unity Game Engine. Shooting causes a projectile to fire which travels until it hits a surface, causing an explosion. The only means to navigate the maps are to ride the wakes of these explosions. This game is very difficult as the player must calculate where and when to fire these projectiles, as the maps require precise movement, and any miscalculation will result in the death of the character. You can play it here.

(character is propelled away from the explosion a proportional speed from the distance of the explosion)
